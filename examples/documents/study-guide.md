# Cellular Respiration Study Guide

## Core Question

How do cells convert chemical energy from food into ATP?

## Atomic Concepts

- Cellular respiration is the process cells use to harvest energy from glucose and related molecules.
- ATP is the cell's short-term energy currency.
- Glycolysis breaks glucose into smaller molecules in the cytosol.
- The citric acid cycle extracts high-energy electrons from fuel-derived molecules.
- The electron transport chain uses electron flow to pump protons across a membrane.
- ATP synthase uses the proton gradient to make ATP.
